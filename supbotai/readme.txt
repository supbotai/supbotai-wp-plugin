== Description ==

"Plug Into ChatGPT's Power" - Seamlessly integrate the prowess of ChatGPT into your WordPress website. Our plugin allows you to offer 24/7 AI-powered customer support, making interactions with your site visitors smooth, efficient, and cost-effective. Elevate your customer service and keep your visitors engaged with instant, intelligent responses.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/supbotai` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings->Plugin Name screen to configure the plugin.
4. Create a free account on supbot.ai and create your project.
5. Copy your Project Key from your project page at supbot.ai and paste it into the required field in the plugin settings.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to create an account on supbot.ai? =

Yes, a free account on supbot.ai is required to generate your unique Project Key, which connects your WordPress site to the AI engine.

= Is the plugin free to use? =

The plugin is free to download and use, with premium plans available for enhanced features and higher volumes of customer interactions.
